Blackjack / 21 game 

Dealer vs user

This game is played using cards from 2 to 10 as face values and J,Q,K as value 10 and A value as 1 or 11

If the total card values is 21 then the one who got 21 wins 

If the total card values is greater than 21 then the player loses 

If both the players got card values more than 21 then the greater card value of the player loses the lesser card value of the player wins

If both the players has card values less than 21 then the player who has greater value is considered to be the winner

If any player who has the card value which is less than 17 (16 or under) then the player have to take a extra cards until the card value reaches more than 16
